Free-Form Flows

December 30, 2024 Free-form flows are a new class of generative models. They are fast, flexible, and can be used for a wide range of applications. They are inspired by normalizing flows, but do away with the inflexible architecture. There are currently three papers that have been published on the topic:
  • Free-Form Flows: Make Any Architecture a Normalizing Flow
  • Lifting Architectural Constraints of Injective Flows
  • Learning Distributions on Manifolds with Free-Form Flows
The code to reproduce the experiments in the papers is available on GitHub.
